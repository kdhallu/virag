ABOUT US

We are India's first napkin manufacturer to use European high end technology and to produce the best in class premium napkins in India. With an automated 4 colors napkin converting line producing napkins on an industrial scale, we take pride in raising Indian consumer expectations and experience for their dining experience. We use FSC (trademark to be put) Certified products and are the only Indian company to focus on exports market for FSC certified tableware products.



MANAGEMENT TEAM

His passion and conviction led hi to establish the largest napkin distribution business in India.
